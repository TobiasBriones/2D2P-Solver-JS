Repsymo is applied mathematical software aiming to solve domain-specific models with granular representation standards.

The current version is implementing several deterministic dynamic programming models to solve many real-world problems in operations research.

Even though the app is still in its initial MVP (Minimum Viable Project) versions, some representations, like the solutions tree, are already state-of-the-art.

Repsymo MVP is open-source software licensed under the GPLv3.0 license. Don't hesitate to contact me if you are interested in these models or wish to extend the application for personal or educational usage.

The early MVP versions of the app were also intended to improve the teaching of operations research and make experimental studies of math solvers, CAS, and arbitrarily complex systems. This was to get the solver at a production level as a long-term project.
